eShopCo Bandwidth Reduction Case Study

This page showcases the work completed for the eShopCo bandwidth reduction case study, focusing on lossless image compression to achieve file sizes under 400 bytes.

The goal was to losslessly compress a challenging image to less than 400 bytes to reduce page load times and cut bandwidth costs.
